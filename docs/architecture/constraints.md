# Architecture Constraints

List constraints that shape architectural decisions.

## Hard Constraints

- Platform requirements.
- Compliance or regulatory needs.
- Performance or scalability limits.

## Soft Constraints

- Preferred tooling and libraries.
- Team conventions or standards.
- Operational requirements.

## What to Capture

- Security, privacy, and compliance needs.
- Performance budgets and reliability targets.
- Environment limitations or deployment policies.
- Data retention or locality requirements.
